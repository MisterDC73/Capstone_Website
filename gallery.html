Core Subsystems

Wall-ECE integrates five specialized subsystems, each solving critical challenges in autonomous disaster response:

📡

Sensing

Vision & Environmental Awareness

Advanced sensor fusion combining color detection, time-of-flight distance sensing, and gyroscope data to give Wall-ECE real-time awareness of its environment.

Components: TCS34725 Color Sensor, VL6180 ToF, MPU6050 Gyroscope
🛞

Locomotion

Omnidirectional Movement

Mecanum wheel system enabling precise, tight-space navigation. Can move within 1 inch of walls—critical for operating in debris-filled environments.

Components: L298N Motor Driver, 330RPM DC Motors, Mecanum Wheels
🎨

Chip Handling

Color Sorting System

Servo-driven mechanism that separates colored chips (Red, Green, Yellow, Blue) with 90%+ accuracy. Fast, reliable, and designed for high-speed operations.

Components: MG90S Servos, PCA9685 Servo Driver, DC-DC Power Module
🦆

Duck Wrangling

Large Object Collection

High-torque system designed to capture and transport rubber ducks without damage. Uses continuous rotation and grip-enhancing materials for reliable handling.

Components: Greartisan 12V Motor, 250 RPM Torque, Nonslip Liner, Rubber Bands
📦

Pedestal Stacking

Precision Placement System

Automated gate and positioning system that identifies colored pedestals and places them in correct order. Combines color sensing with precise servo control.

Components: MG90S Servos, TCS34725 Color Sensor, Positioning Gates
🧠

Control Center

Central Coordination

Arduino GIGA R1 WiFi microcontroller orchestrating all subsystems. 76 digital pins, 12 PWM channels, and optimized C/C++ firmware for autonomous decision-making.

Components: Arduino GIGA R1 WiFi, 3 I2C Ports, Low Power Design
