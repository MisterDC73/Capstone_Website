Wall-ECE

Autonomous Disaster Cleanup Robot

Via the Mississippi State University Hardware Competition

Wall-ECE is an innovative proof-of-concept robot designed to aid first responders in disaster zones. Our system autonomously detects, collects, sorts, and places debris with precision and speed.

Featuring advanced sensing, omnidirectional locomotion, and intelligent material handling, Wall-ECE demonstrates the future of automated disaster response.
